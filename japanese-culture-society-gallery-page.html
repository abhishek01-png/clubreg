Japanese Language Workshop

Throughout the workshop, we will cover various aspects of the Japanese language, including vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and basic conversation skills. Participants will have the opportunity to practice their language skills through interactive activities, role-plays, group discussions, and language exercises.
Event

Shogi Workshop

During the workshop, participants will have the opportunity to learn the basic rules of shogi, including how each piece moves and captures, and the objective of the game. We will provide hands-on practice and guidance, allowing everyone to gain a practical understanding of the game.
Event

Cosplay Workshop

During the workshop, participants will have the opportunity to delve into the world of cosplay, from the initial concept to the final costume creation. Our experienced cosplayers and costume designers will guide and inspire participants, sharing their knowledge and techniques to help bring their favorite characters to life.
